History Lima Horse
Lima Horse is recently founded by Mr. Diederik Binnema, a horse lover who wants to help you find your perfect horse. Lima Horse is located in the north of the Netherlands from which our services are delivered. Lima Horse finds its customers the most important and will deliver excellent service. In addition to selling horses, we also provide intensive training for horse and its rider. Our extensive network and many years of experience give us the opportunity to help you find your perfect horse and, if desired,the horse-riding training at your own level.


Diederik Binnema
Diederik earned his reputation in the equestrian industry by exporting horses and recieving specific training in Europe. He saw the growing demand from China and decided to expand hisbusiness to this country.
Diederik rode his own horses at several championships, winning different titles on national and international level. He also gained high equestrian experiences in foreign countries. He used to work for Andy Austin, an international show jumper in Great Britain. Then, Diederik expanded his experience in Australia, working on a horse station. His main responsibility was in the field of horse breeding and veterinarian services. Afterwards, he experienced practical horse riding under rough conditions, as a cowboy on a ranch in the United States of America.
To this day, horses have been a vital part of Diederik's life. In the field of trading business, Diederik is very experienced with different kinds of East Asian countries such as Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia and the Philippines. Since 2006 he has also been visiting China for business purposes on a regular basis (3 - 4 times a year). Several Chinese business agents have become his close and reliable partners since then.
